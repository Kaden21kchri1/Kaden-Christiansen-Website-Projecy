About Me!

My name is Kaden Christiansen! I grew up in Oregon and am now living in Utah, attending BYU. I found a passion for photography when I was a freshman in high school. I first found interest in drones, then drones with cameras. Finally, I loved taking videos and photos with my drone so much that I eventually got myself a DSLR canon camera. My best work is taking and making videos, but I loved doing photography on the side, it takes a lot less work and I love just capturing moments through how I see. I love to bring out my creativity and make my work unique from everyone else.
